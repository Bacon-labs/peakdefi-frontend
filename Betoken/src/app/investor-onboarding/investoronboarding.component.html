Frequently asked questions

Can I buy and sell Betoken Shares at any time?

Yes. Betoken's protocol is 100% liquid. You can buy and sell BTKS at any time in 2 ways:
  1. During Betoken's management phase (27 days), BTKS is freely tradable on exchanges. The price may be different from what's displayed on the investor dashboard.
  2. During Betoken's intermission phase (3 days), you can buy and sell BTKS directly on the investor dashboard.

How can I buy Betoken Shares?

Click on the Get Started button and follow the instructions. You'll be able to use Ether or any of our 50+ supported ERC-20 tokens to buy Betoken Shares (BTKS).

Do you charge fees?

Yes. We're charging the following monthly fees to pay our managers and pay for the development & maintainance of Betoken.

  • Performance fee: 20% (of profits)
  • Management fee: 0.2% (of assets)

What are the performances of the Betoken fund?

You can check our performance metrics on our website and investor dashboard.
